My Favourite Recipes

This is a collection of my favourite recipes

Brownies

List of ingredients

  • Flour
  • Nutella
  • Eggs

Steps:

  1. Put flour into the bowl
  2. Crack 3 eggs
  3. Pour 250g of nutella in the bowl
  4. Mix it
  5. Pour the mixture into a baking pan
  6. Bake at 175 degree celsius for 15 minutes
  7. Enjoy!

Pancakes

List of ingredients

  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Eggs
  • Water
  • Oil

Steps:

  1. Put the 1 cup flour into a bowl
  2. Add the 1/2 cup sugar
  3. Add the 2 eggs
  4. Add 1/4 cup of oil
  5. Add water
  6. Mix it well
  7. Heat a little oil in the pan
  8. Fry the mixture bit by bit
  9. Serve hot

Vanilla Cake

List of ingredients

  • Flour
  • Butter
  • Sugar
  • Eggs
  • Vanilla extract
  • Baking Powder

Steps:

  1. Put 250g of butter and 220g of sugar into a bowl
  2. Mix well until light and fluffy
  3. In another bowl, break 3 large eggs, add vanilla extract and whisk
  4. Pour the whisked egg into the butter and sugar mixture
  5. Pour in 300g of flour and 1tbsp of baking powder
  6. Mix it by folding in
  7. Butter the pan and pour in the mixture
  8. Bake

Puff-Puff

List of ingredients

  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Yeast
  • Water
  • Oil

Steps:

  1. Put 250g of flour in a bowl
  2. Put 50g of sugar into the same bowl
  3. Add 3tbsp of yeast
  4. Add 1 cup of water and then mix
  5. Heat the oil in a pan
  6. Then deep-fry the puffs until golden brown
  7. Serve hot

Buns

List of ingredients

  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Baking Powder
  • Butter
  • Water
  • Oil

Steps:

  1. Put 250g of flour into a bowl
  2. Put 40g of sugar into the bowl
  3. Add 1/2tbsp of baking powder
  4. Add 20g of butter and blend into the dry mixture
  5. Then add 1 cup of water and then mix
  6. Heat the oil in a pan
  7. Then deep-fry the buns until golden brown
  8. Serve hot
